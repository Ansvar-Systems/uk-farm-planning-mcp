# Disclaimer

This MCP server provides UK farm planning data for informational purposes only.

## Not Professional Advice

The data served by this tool does not constitute professional agricultural, financial, tax, or legal advice. Crop rotation guidance, gross margins, tax rules, APR guidance, tenancy law summaries, and diversification information are based on published UK government, AHDB, HMRC, and statutory sources. Individual circumstances vary. Always consult a qualified agricultural accountant for tax matters, an agricultural solicitor for tenancy disputes, and a RICS-qualified surveyor for land valuations.

## Data Sources

All data is sourced from:

- **AHDB Farmbench / Pocketbook** -- Agriculture and Horticulture Development Board
- **HMRC Manuals and Guidance** -- Crown Copyright, HM Revenue & Customs
- **UK Legislation** -- Agricultural Holdings Act 1986, Agricultural Tenancies Act 1995
- **Town and Country Planning (General Permitted Development) Order 2015** -- Crown Copyright

All data is used under the [Open Government Licence v3](https://www.nationalarchives.gov.uk/doc/open-government-licence/version/3/) where applicable. Legislation text is Crown Copyright.

## Currency

Tax rules, APR thresholds, and gross margins change. This tool shows the data available at the time of its last ingestion run. Tax rules are especially volatile -- always check current HMRC guidance before making decisions. Check `check_data_freshness` for the age of the data.
